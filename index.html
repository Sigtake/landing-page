How it works

Live in three steps

01

Connect your systems

Create an API key and push your first alert with a single request.

curl -X POST api.sigtake.com/api/ingest \
  -H "X-Api-Key: sk_9f3a1c04e2" \
  -d '{"title":"High CPU on web-01",
      "severity":"critical",
      "source":"datadog-agent"}'
02

Route to teams & channels

Each key belongs to a team. Assign channels once and every alert finds its way to email, SMS, Teams or in-app.

Infrastructure → Email + Teams
Payments → Email + SMS
Platform → In-app
03

Acknowledge, resolve, improve

Work alerts as a team and watch your median time-to-acknowledge trend down, week over week.
